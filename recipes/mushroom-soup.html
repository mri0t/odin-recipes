Mushroom Soup

Straight from Estonia

Delicious earthy soup that fills you right up to the brim

Make it ,eat it and make more to eat more.

Ingredients

  • Glass of barley groats
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Mushrooms
  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt, Dill, Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook groats till they are soft
  2. Heat some oil, add sliced onions,mushrooms and tomato.
  3. Cook, while onion and tomato are half ready
  4. Add potato slices, groats and onion mix into the soup
  5. Cook, till the potato is soft and season it
  6. Serve with sour cream and add a lot of dills for that extra nature
