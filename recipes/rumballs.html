Rum Balls

Most amazing balls of all time

Simple yet amazing

Best for when you don't have time to make desserts for the weekend as they don't take much besides taking room in the fridge!

Ingredients

  • Crushed biscuits
  • Sweetened condendsed milk
  • Coconut
  • Cocoa
  • Rum

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients
  2. Wet hands and roll the combined mixture into small balls
  3. Coat the balls in coconut
  4. Refrigerate or freeze them for the final superb taste
